My Playground
Tutored project IMAC 2

My Playground is an application developped in order to encourage the sociolization of students between 8 and 11 years old.


My role in the project : projet manager

Context :

This application was made during the three weeks of tutored project in IMAC 2.

Motivated by the difficulty that students encounter with online classes, Design Insitu agency, based in Istanbul, asked us to conceive an innovative technology,
in order to help young children to acquire the basis of a solid sociolization.

Indeed, the current pandemic made the social contact hard. Kids now only reunite for online classes.

That's why it was important for us to conceive an application which would allow them to simulate real activities, through the use of Virtual Reality.

Interactive logo
Esthetic Algorithm IMAC 1

During the first year in IMAC, we had the chance to go to the ZKM Museum in Germany to see an exhibition called "Writing the History of the Future"

Later in the semester, we had to reinvent the logo of the exhibition, using processing, p5.js.

With my classmate, we decided to use the typography and work on the letters.

We did a lot of research about the typographies over the years and we desided to use tbe sound.

Indeed, the louder people get around the computer, the faster the typography changes.
